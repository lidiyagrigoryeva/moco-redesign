Memberships

Aquatics Pass

Swimmers will appreciate this pass, as it offers year-round access to the County’s four indoor pools, along with entrance to the seven outdoor pools that are only open in the summer months -- from Memorial Day Weekend through Labor Day Weekend. Discounted pricing is available to families, couples and adults who are ages 55+, and budget friendly 12-admission pass options are also available. All pass holders must reside at the same address.

Center Pass

Montgomery County Recreation fitness passes for exercise enthusiasts are free for County residents 2023 through 2024. The free pass provides access to fully-equipped fitness rooms, open gym (drop-in) activities, and game rooms at any Community Recreation Center during regularly scheduled hours.

To apply for a fitness pass, visit a center location. Proof of residency (a photo ID with an address or a photo ID and a current utility bill) must be provided to qualify for the free pass. Please note, youth ages 14-15 with a valid resident youth pass are able to use a fitness room only when accompanied by a parent/guardian with a valid fitness pass. The fitness pass does not include access to aquatic centers. You must have a photo on file with your membership before using any facility.

Senior Center Pass

Montgomery County Residents over the age of 55 are welcome to use all amenities, such as fitness centers and billiard rooms, in all senior center facilities free of charge. Unless otherwise stated, classes and programs are also free in Montgomery County Recreation Senior Center facilities. Upon first-time arrival, staff will ask for proof of Montgomery County residency (such as an ID) and have you fill out a form, granting you a physical membership card for unlimited access to all senior centers in Montgomery County. Senior center passes are currently unavailable for non-residents.

Inclement Weather

When Montgomery County Recreation must close or cancel programs due to inclement weather or other circumstances related to participant and staff safety, the first notification is made through Montgomery County’s emergency notification system, Alert Montgomery.

If you would like to receive free emergency alerts for Montgomery County, sign up for Alert Montgomery.
