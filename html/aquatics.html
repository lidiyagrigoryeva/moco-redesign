Aquatics

Montgomery County Recreation offers everything you need —instruction, competition, fitness, safety and fun. Year-round aquatics programs for all ages. Check out our extensive program offerings below.

Competitive Programs

Take on some fierce competition in one of Montgomery County Recreation's competitive swim programs.

Diving

Diving consists of a diver jumping from a springboard or platform and performing acrobatic maneuvers before plunging into water.

Safety Training

Montgomery County Recreation offers casses in Lifeguard Training, Pool Operator, and CPR/FRP/AED.

Scuba

SCUBA stands for "Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus" which is exactly what you'll learn to use in our SCUBA programs.

Swim Lessons

Our aquatic facilities offers instruction for all ages and swimming abilities.

Water Fitness

We have a variety of programs that offer workouts to help you get fit and feel great, while building cardiovascular fitness and strength.

Inclement Weather

When Montgomery County Recreation must close or cancel programs due to inclement weather or other circumstances related to participant and staff safety, the first notification is made through Montgomery County’s emergency notification system, Alert Montgomery.

If you would like to receive free emergency alerts for Montgomery County, sign up for Alert Montgomery.
