Programs For Adults 55+

Montgomery County Recreation has many ways for independent adults, 55 and older, to stay active and healthy. Whether you are working, semi-retired, or fully retired, our eight senior centers offer many programs, activities, classes, and events for you to choose from. Our centers have diverse amenities that may include fitness rooms, social halls, meeting spaces, classrooms, billiards and game rooms.

Day Trips

Montgomery County Recreation provides several programs for individuals 55+ to travel throughout the Washington and mid-Atlantic Region to enjoy the sights, sounds, history, culture, and outdoors.

Free Classes & Programs

We are pleased to offer Montgomery County residents over the age of 55 the opportunity to participate in hundreds of classes and programs of all varieties at no cost! Call or visit your nearest senior center for more information.

Maryland Senior Olympics

Montgomery County Recreation is the host agency for the Maryland Senior Olympics (MSO), with many of the events happening right here in Montgomery County!

Senior Nutrition

Montgomery County offers a hot meal at lunchtime at 10 senior program locations around the County. This program is offered in partnership with the Department of Health & Human Services, Aging and Disability Services. Please check with the center nearest you for more information.

Senior Transportation

Montgomery County Recreation provides offer a variety of transportation programs and services to help seniors get around the county. Many of them are free or have a very small fee. Please check with the center nearest you for more information.
