Camps

Montgomery County Recreation offers a wide variety of exciting summer programs throughout the County with an emphasis on safety, convenience, affordability and most of all fun! We offer summer programs designed to appeal to a variety of campers. With such a broad range of programming, and experienced and innovative staff, there is something for everyone.

Arts, Dance, & Music

Explore a variety of fun and creative indoor-oriented activities.

General

Enjoy a variety of organized and supervised activities. Indoor and outdoor camps are available.

Special Interest

Special interest camps provide exciting and enriching experiences for campers ages 5-15, including cooking, chess, STEAM and more.

Sports

Sports camps are designed for those who enjoy enthusiastic and athletic play. Learn new skills, teamwork, good sportsmanship and stay active.

Teen SSL

These camps offer students the opportunity to earn their mandated Student Service Learning (SSL) hours and learn new skills while having fun.

Therapeutic Rec

Enjoy a variety of summer camps available for children/teens ages 6 - 21 with disabilities.
