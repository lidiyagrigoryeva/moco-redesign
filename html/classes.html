Classes & Programs

Discover hundreds of exciting classes, clinics and workshops we offer throughout Montgomery County.

Arts & Crafts

Explore your creativity with us. We offer a variety of art classes including pottery, painting, and more.

Cooking

Discover your inner culinary genius with a variety of classes for all skill levels.

Dance

It's time to get moving! We offer a large variety of dance classes for all ages and fitness levels.

Strengthen, tone, and build muscle in our fitness classes for adults of all ages and fitness levels.
