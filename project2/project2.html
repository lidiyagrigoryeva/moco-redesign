Project 2: Website Proposal

Client and Topic

The project involves redesigning the existing Montgomery County Department of Recreation's website, focusing on user engagement and content relevancy to serve the community effectively.

Development Process and Engagement

As the website designer for Montgomery County Recreation, I am collaborating closely with my client, my employer. The development process involves active participation from representatives of the director's office, business services, communications, IT, youth development, and senior programs. Their valuable insights are integral to redesigning a website that reflects the department's mission and addresses the community's diverse needs. Input from each sub-department will ensure the website comprehensively represents all offered recreation services.

Testing

Before launching, we will conduct thorough user testing with department staff and a wide range of community members, focusing especially on seniors to ensure the website is user-friendly and accessible. This testing will assess task completion, ease of navigation, and readability on different devices, ensuring the website meets user needs and provides an inclusive experience.

Browser testing will cover a range of the most popular web browsers to ensure compatibility and performance across different platforms. This will include the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple's Safari, and Microsoft Edge. Testing on these browsers will help identify and resolve any issues that could impact user experience, ensuring that the website is accessible and functional for the widest possible audience. Additionally, older versions of these browsers will be considered to accommodate users who may not have updated their software.

Description

Montgomery County Recreation is committed to providing a variety of high-quality, diverse, and accessible programs, services, and facilities, aimed at enhancing the quality of life for all ages, cultures, and abilities in Montgomery County, Maryland. The goal of this business is to foster community engagement, physical health, and well-being through its recreational offerings.

The website for Montgomery County Recreation is designed to be a central informational hub, showcasing the range of available programs, services, and facilities to the community. It serves as an accessible and navigable platform for residents to find and register for various recreational activities.

The target audience of the website is the diverse population of Montgomery County, encompassing individuals of all ages, cultural and ethnic backgrounds, and varying levels of economic and educational status. The design of the website will take into consideration the varied digital literacy levels and the different devices used by the community, ensuring a responsive and inclusive user experience.

In developing the website, the diversity of the target audience is a key consideration, influencing the creation of an intuitive and user-friendly interface. Accessibility features and ADA compliance will be prioritized to cater to users with different abilities. The website will feature unique content that I will create, including a user-friendly interface, written program descriptions, a dynamic events calendar, and visually engaging elements that reflect the community's spirit. Additional content, such as logos and images, will be sourced from the graphic design and communications department, along with contributions from partner organizations.

Content updates on the website will be a regular task to ensure the information remains current and relevant. Major content refreshes, such as program schedules and event details, will occur monthly, while critical updates like service changes or emergency notices will be posted as necessary. The implementation of a content management system will facilitate efficient and timely updates, keeping the website a reliable and up-to-date resource for the community.

Growth and Maintenance

Montgomery County Recreation website will be designed to keep pace with the evolving nature of community services and programs. It will be built on scalable architecture, allowing for the easy addition of new features like interactive maps and online events. The plan includes regularly scheduled maintenance and updates: content will be updated monthly and technical enhancements will be applied as they become available. A key part of my strategy is to implement a robust feedback system, enabling continuous improvement based on user input. Regular maintenance tasks will involve updating program information, monitoring user feedback, and optimizing site performance. To ensure the website remains relevant and efficient, user feedback will be checked daily, perform minor updates weekly, and conduct comprehensive content reviews monthly.

Organization

Homepage Wireframe

The homepage wireframe for the Montgomery County Department of Recreation website features a well-organized and intuitive design. At the very top, a navigation bar provides easy and straightforward access to the main sections of the website, ensuring that users can quickly find what they are looking for. Below the navigation bar, there is a captivating rotating header with a series of images. These images, which transition smoothly from one to the next after a set time interval, showcase various activities and facilities offered by the department, immediately engaging visitors with dynamic visual content. Further enhancing user experience, especially for those who prefer not to use the navigation bar, are quick link icons positioned below the header. These icons serve as an efficient visual guide, allowing direct access to key areas of the site with just a click. Finally, the bottom section of the homepage is dedicated to a list-style calendar of events. This feature provides a clear and organized overview of upcoming programs and events, encouraging community involvement and participation in department activities. The overall layout of the homepage wireframe is thoughtfully designed to combine aesthetic appeal with functional ease, making it welcoming and accessible to users of all ages and abilities.

Secondary Wireframe Template

The template wireframe for the secondary pages of the Montgomery County Department of Recreation website maintains a consistent and user-friendly layout, ensuring a cohesive user experience throughout the site. Similar to the homepage, each page features a navigation bar at the top, providing consistent and easy access to other sections of the website. Directly below the navigation bar is a dedicated header that displays images relevant to each specific page, offering a visual cue to the users about the page's content. A key feature of this template is the inclusion of three aside boxes situated alongside the main content area. These boxes are versatile and adaptive, containing information pertinent to the specific page. For instance, on a page about a community center, these asides might display the center's hours of operation, contact information, downloadable resources, inclement weather updates, and links to social media platforms. This arrangement not only enriches the page with useful and relevant information but also ensures that important details like contact and operational information are easily accessible without cluttering the main content area. This design approach enhances both the functionality and the aesthetic appeal of the pages, making them informative yet uncluttered.

Security

Adding an e-commerce feature for payments on the Montgomery County Department of Recreation's website requires a strong, varied security approach. The website will have secure payment gateways with SSL encryption to protect financial data and meet Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) requirements. Users making transactions will need to create accounts, which will be secured with strong passwords and possibly two-factor authentication for added safety.

As the designer and developer, I'll actively work to strengthen the website's security. This includes regularly updating the website and e-commerce software to fix vulnerabilities, encrypting data beyond SSL for transactions to secure user account information, and using web application firewalls to prevent threats and unauthorized access. Continuous monitoring systems will be set up to detect suspicious activities, with automated alerts for any security issues.

The checkout process will be both user-friendly and secure to reduce the risk of data theft. Working together with the IT department, regular security audits will be performed to find and fix any weaknesses and to set up a strong backup and recovery system for quick recovery in case of a breach or system failure. There will also be focus on educating users about protecting their accounts and recognizing phishing attempts. With these measures, the website will be a safe, trustworthy place for community members to register and pay for recreational programs, keeping their personal and financial information secure.

Web Hosting

Given that this is a redesign of an existing site, the host must support the specific technologies and infrastructure currently in use. This includes compatibility with the existing content management system and any special technologies such as SSL for secure connections; this is particularly important due to the website's e-commerce capabilities for class and program payments.

The web host must offer robust security features to protect sensitive user data, especially since the site handles financial transactions. Features like regular backups, firewall protection, and malware scanning are crucial. Additionally, scalability is important to accommodate future growth in site traffic and content. Reliable uptime and responsive customer support are also critical factors, ensuring that the website remains accessible so that issues can be promptly addressed.

The website, being a government entity, would benefit from a host that specializes in or has experience with government websites, particularly recreation, ensuring compliance with relevant standards and regulations. Given the variety of content and functionalities, the website will need to support HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to ensure a dynamic and responsive user experience. Server-side scripting languages like PHP and a database system such as MySQL are necessary for managing the e-commerce components and user data. These choices are driven by the need for robust, flexible, and widely-supported technologies that can handle the diverse requirements of a community recreation website.

Since this project is a redesign of an exisiting website, montgomerycountymd.gov/rec/ is Montgomery County Recreation's current domain, with mocorec.com serving as a shortened version. The existing domains are well-established and aptly represent the department, with the shortened version being particularly effective for social media and marketing purposes due to its brevity and clear reference to Montgomery County Recreation.

Marketing

The Montgomery County Department of Recreation's website marketing will use a multi-channel approach. Social media like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram will be key, with both organic posts and ads to attract a broad audience. The department will share engaging content like videos, images, and user stories to boost visibility and draw people to the website. Additionally, newsletters and flyers will be used in community, swim, and senior centers for local outreach. During summer, when hiring increases for swim centers, the department will include website promotions in job advertisements, directing potential employees and the public to the site for information and program registration.

For effective Search Engine Optimization (SEO), several strategies are incorporated into the design and content of the Montgomery County Department of Recreation website. The content strategically includes keywords related to recreation and community services in Montgomery County to improve search engine rankings. Engaging and regularly updated content, such as health articles, program updates, and community stories, attracts visitors and encourages longer site engagement, thus reducing bounce rates. The website's responsive, mobile-friendly design is another key factor in enhancing SEO. Image optimization, use of alt tags, clear and keyword-rich meta descriptions and titles, and building backlinks through local organizational partnerships further bolster the site's visibility and authority in search engine results.
